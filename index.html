About me

Diego Garcia is a talented filmmaker and editor born in Colombia, but raised in New York. Throughout his career, Diego has honed his craft and gained recognition for his innovative and visually stunning work.

Diego's passion for filmmaking began during his time studying journalism and media at Mercy College in New York. He continued his education at the School of Visual Arts, where he refined his skills in filmmaking and editing. Later, he pursued further studies at the American Intercontinental University in London, where he was able to expand his international perspective and hone his technical abilities.

Over the years, Diego has worked on a variety of projects, ranging from short films, music videos, documentaries and commercials. He has earned a reputation for his technical expertise, creative vision and attention to detail.  

Diego's passion for storytelling, combined with his technical expertise and commitment to excellence, make him a standout talent in the world of filmmaking. Whether he is working on a short film, a documentary, or a commercial project, he brings a unique perspective and a commitment to delivering outstanding results.
